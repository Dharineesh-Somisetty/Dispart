About the Event

Unwind after a long week with our beginner-friendly pottery class! We'll provide all the clay, tools, and a glass of natural wine to get your creative juices flowing.

You'll learn basic hand-building techniques to create your own unique mug or bowl. No prior experience needed—just bring good vibes and clothes you don't mind getting a little dusty!
